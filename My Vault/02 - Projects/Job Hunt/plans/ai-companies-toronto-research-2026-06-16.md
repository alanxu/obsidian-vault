>
> **Score definitions:**
> - **Influence (1–3) — calibrated to a 20/30/50 distribution:**
>   - **3** = Top ~20% of the AI ecosystem. Market-defining. Public mega-caps + frontier model labs that define the field.
>   - **2** = Middle ~30%. Well-known, established, but not defining the field.
>   - **1** = Bottom ~50%. Niche / vertical / early-stage. Includes the cool-but-not-market-defining startups.
>   - **Banks = always 1**, even if they have a dedicated AI lab.
> - **Toronto (0–2):**
>   - **0** = No Toronto office, no Remote-CA hiring, no Canada presence
>   - **1** = Remote-CA / US-only but has some Canada presence (recruiter reachouts, visa sponsorship, US offices accessible)
>   - **2** = Toronto office, major engineering presence, or HQ in Toronto
