**Notes:**
- Big Tech (Google, Meta, Microsoft, NVIDIA) Toronto comp usually matches US levels in USD, often higher than local banks
- Banks (RBC, TD, BMO, Scotia) pay slightly below Big Tech but offer strong work-life balance
- Cohere pays competitively with US AI labs
- Toronto has a "Big 5" banks + Shopify + Cohere + Tenstorrent + Vector + academic core (UofT, York, Vector, Mila pipeline)

### Most In-Demand Skills (Toronto AI)
1. **LLM application engineering** — RAG, agent orchestration, prompt eval
2. **Python + PyTorch/JAX** — still the lingua franca
3. **Distributed training / inference** — vLLM, TensorRT-LLM, Triton
4. **Multimodal systems** — vision + language integration
5. **MLOps / Eval** — building production pipelines with evaluation harnesses
6. **Safety / alignment** — especially for foundation model work

### Toronto AI Ecosystem Highlights
- **Vector Institute** — main industry-academic bridge
- **UofT, York, Waterloo** — top ML feeder programs
- **MaRS Discovery District** — startup hub where many AI companies have offices
- **Toronto's "AI supercluster"** status (Pan-Canadian AI Strategy)
- Strong **Big 5 bank** AI labs (RBC Borealis, TD Layer 6, BMO, Scotia, CIBC)
- Lower cost of living than SF, but Big Tech comp is US-equivalent
